About us

A trusted partner
across markets.

Brookstone Advisory is a Hong Kong-based advisory firm with deep expertise in international markets. We work with multinational corporations, family offices, and emerging businesses seeking to operate, expand, or invest across borders.

Our team brings together decades of experience in corporate strategy, regulatory affairs, and cross-border transactions spanning Asia, Europe, and the Americas. We combine on-the-ground market knowledge with a global perspective — helping clients move decisively in complex environments.

We take a relationship-first approach: every engagement is built on candid counsel, rigorous analysis, and a genuine commitment to our clients' long-term success. We are fully independent — we carry no conflicts of interest and no hidden agendas.

What we do

Our services.

We offer a focused range of advisory services for organisations operating in or expanding into international markets. Every engagement is tailored — we do not offer off-the-shelf solutions.

Corporate Strategy & Market Entry

Rigorous market assessment, competitive positioning, and go-to-market planning for businesses entering new geographies or restructuring their international operations.

Cross-Border Transactions

End-to-end advisory on mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures, and strategic partnerships across jurisdictions — from deal structuring to due diligence and negotiation support.

Regulatory & Government Affairs

Guidance on navigating complex regulatory environments, licensing requirements, and government relations across Asia and beyond, with a focus on practical, actionable outcomes.

Capital & Investment Advisory

Supporting founders, family offices, and institutional investors in structuring capital raises, identifying strategic partners, and evaluating cross-border investment opportunities.

Risk & Geopolitical Analysis

In-depth assessments of political, regulatory, and macroeconomic risks affecting international business — helping clients anticipate change and build resilient strategies.

Board & Executive Advisory

Confidential counsel to boards and senior leadership on strategic decisions, governance, and stakeholder management in complex, multi-jurisdictional environments.
