About us

Independent expertise.
Real-world results.

Brookstone Advisory Limited is a Hong Kong-based professional advisory firm established to help businesses improve financial management efficiency, optimise capital deployment, and strengthen risk management capabilities.

Our directors bring a combined 30 years of hands-on experience in financial services and corporate advisory, having served clients across a broad range of industries and business sizes. Our expertise spans corporate treasury management, foreign exchange risk, cross-border fund arrangements, and financial planning.

We work with SMEs, import and export companies, cross-border businesses, technology firms, Web3 enterprises, and professional services companies across Hong Kong, Australia, and the United Kingdom. Every engagement is fully independent — we carry no conflicts of interest and no hidden agendas.

What we do

Our services.

We provide focused, specialist advisory services for businesses that need expert guidance on treasury, foreign exchange, accounting, and digital asset finance. Every engagement is tailored to the client's specific situation.

Corporate Treasury Advisory

We help businesses optimise how they manage and deploy capital — improving financial efficiency and planning for growth.

  • Cash management and liquidity planning
  • Cross-border fund arrangement advisory
  • Working capital optimisation
  • Financial process improvement
Corporate FX Risk Management

We advise businesses exposed to foreign currency volatility on how to identify, assess, and manage their FX risk effectively.

  • FX risk exposure assessment
  • Hedging strategy recommendations
  • Multi-currency cash flow planning
  • FX policy design and review
Corporate Accounting Advisory

We support finance teams with financial reporting, management accounting, and the optimisation of accounting processes and controls.

  • Financial reporting support
  • Management accounting advisory
  • Accounting process optimisation
  • Internal controls and compliance guidance
Web3 & Blockchain Advisory

We provide specialist accounting and finance advisory for businesses operating in the digital asset and blockchain space — an area requiring dedicated expertise.

  • Digital asset financial management
  • Blockchain enterprise accounting
  • Web3 compliance and reporting
  • Token and crypto treasury advisory
Where we work

Our markets.

We currently serve corporate clients across Hong Kong, Australia, and the United Kingdom, with a vision to expand into further markets as we grow. Our focus is on businesses operating cross-border between Asia, Oceania, and Europe.

Hong Kong

Our home base and primary market. We work with local SMEs, trading companies, and multinational businesses headquartered or operating in Hong Kong.

Australia

We advise Australian businesses expanding into Asia, as well as Asia-based businesses with Australian operations or investment interests.

United Kingdom

We support UK-based businesses with cross-border treasury and FX needs, and Hong Kong companies with existing operations or investment interests in the United Kingdom.
